About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

We, Royal Food Court, situated at Savitri Vihar, Nagpur, Maharashtra, offer pre-planned meal packages that a customer can customize to his liking.

We offer a wide array of meal options in vegetarian and non-vegetarian section.
Each option is not just finger-licking good but highly nutritious and prepared under very hygienic conditions.

Royal Food Court is all about door delivering home-cooked meals and homemade delicacies. Each plate of food served through Royal Food Court is prepared using fresh, locally sourced produce in a home kitchen just like yours with authentic, regional recipes by real people with a passion for cooking.
